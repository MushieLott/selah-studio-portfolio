About

Hi, I'm Charlotte.

I'm a designer, illustrator and operations person currently based in Bali, with my heart set on returning to New Zealand — where I lived for four years and built friendships and working relationships I still treasure.

My work sits in an unusual and useful place: I can make a brand genuinely beautiful and keep the engine room running behind it — the accounts, the stock, the systems, the day-to-day that keeps a small business alive. Most people do one or the other. I love doing both.

My faith shapes how I work: with honesty, care, and a real desire to make things that serve people well. Selah — a word from the Psalms meaning to pause and reflect — is the spirit I bring to every project.
